About Us

Medha Fashion is a premium ladies' boutique in Kerala which is started in 2015, founded by Nishidha, one of the best fashion designers in Kerala. In these years we're all about designing premium wedding dresses and celebrating uniqueness in every dress we stitch. We've been trusted with many premium weddings, engagements, baby showers, and other functions and we'll continue to do so with our best designer wedding dresses, Not only weddings Nishida has designed premium dresses for women celebrities for their special occasions. Each premium dress we design is a celebration of individuality and a reflection of love stories and Special occasions.
